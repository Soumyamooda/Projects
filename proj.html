Welcome to Open Source Contribution

Join the community and contribute to open source projects.

Why Contribution to Open Sources?

Contributing to open source projects allows you to collaborate with developers worldwide, improve your skills, gain knowledge and make a positive impact on the software community.

Getting Started

If you're new to open source contribution, you can start and enhance your skills and make projects by the following guidance:

  • Choose a project that interests you.
  • Forking the project's repository on GitHub or another platform.
  • Checking for open issues and picking one to work on.
  • Submitting a pull request with your contributions.
